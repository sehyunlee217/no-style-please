### Some short thoughts in no particular order

- Commuting sucks (2 hours a day takes up 500 hours a year!)
- Regardless, commuting forced me to become a morning person and that I'm pretty happy about.
- I wonder what the buskers at Finch station do outside of commute hours.
- People don't really look outside when getting to work.
- I miss interacting with large groups of people; I thought I would be happy at a more technical position but was very, very wrong.
- I miss school.
- Do people actually enjoy work or work so they can do what they enjoy?
- When do we (or should we) stop worrying about the future?
- More people should thank the bus drivers when getting off.
- I wonder whether our generation will have auditory problems in the future from listening to music too much.
- Running to catch a bus/subway is not worth it but does feel good if you do.
- Not only does bus/subway drivers help our commute, they also have to commute to their jobs as well.
- I still don't understand why the YRT is more expensive than the TTC.
